Our Programs

How we support STEM and literacy education for students, families, and communities.

Bottor STEM & Literacy Innovation Program

The Bottor STEM & Literacy Innovation Program is our flagship initiative — a comprehensive approach to education that brings together hands-on learning, critical thinking, and literacy development in a connected, student-centered experience.

The program provides hands-on learning experiences that help students develop literacy, STEM, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills through project-based activities, interactive technology, and educational resources.

Supporting program areas include:

  • STEM exploration
  • Literacy development
  • Hands-on projects
  • Interactive learning tools
  • Classroom and community partnerships
  • Educational games

STEM Exploration

Science, technology, engineering, and mathematics are not just school subjects — they are ways of thinking and solving problems. Our STEM exploration programming gives students hands-on opportunities to experiment, investigate, and discover.

We support activities and challenges that develop analytical thinking, scientific inquiry, and a genuine curiosity about how the world works — helping students build skills they will use for a lifetime.

  • Science experiments and engineering design challenges
  • Math problem-solving and computational thinking activities
  • Technology exploration and digital skills development

Literacy Development

Strong reading and writing skills are essential to a student's success across every subject area. Our literacy development programming supports foundational reading skills, comprehension, vocabulary, and a genuine love of language and storytelling.

We work to ensure that students — especially those in underserved communities — have access to high-quality literacy instruction and resources during the most critical windows of language development.

  • Foundational reading skills and phonics instruction
  • Reading comprehension and vocabulary development
  • Writing support and literacy-rich learning activities
  • Access to books and reading materials

Hands-on Projects & Project-Based Learning

Students learn best when they are actively engaged — building, creating, experimenting, and solving real problems. Our hands-on project programming uses project-based learning to bring STEM and literacy skills to life.

Through meaningful challenges and creative projects, students develop persistence, collaboration, and the problem-solving skills they need to navigate complex situations both inside and outside the classroom.

  • Engineering design and building challenges
  • Cross-subject projects connecting STEM and literacy
  • Creative problem-solving and innovation activities

Interactive Learning Tools & Technology

Interactive technology is one powerful tool in our educational approach. Digital resources and educational games can deepen engagement, reinforce skills, and make learning accessible in new ways — particularly for students who benefit from varied learning experiences.

We incorporate interactive technology as part of a broader, well-rounded educational experience — always in service of deeper learning, not as an end in itself.

  • Educational games that reinforce STEM and literacy skills
  • Digital tools for at-home and classroom learning
  • Interactive resources that complement hands-on instruction

Classroom & Community Partnerships

Great education does not happen in isolation. Bottor Learning Foundation partners with schools, libraries, and community organizations across Wisconsin to bring STEM and literacy programming directly to students where they live and learn.

Through these partnerships, we work to create environments where learning is supported, celebrated, and accessible — and where families and educators are equipped to reinforce skills at home and in the classroom.

  • School and classroom partnerships across Wisconsin
  • Library and community center programming
  • Educator resources and instructional support
  • Family engagement and take-home learning tools
